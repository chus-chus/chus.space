2022

Research Assistant | U. of Waikato & BarcelonaTech

For ~2 years, I was interned and employed in various ways by the two institutions. Participated in two distinct projects.

  • COPEDI-Cat: A COVID-19 emergency response group by the Catalan government. Many paediatricians collaborated in a network. As part of the BIOCOMSC research group, I was tasked with data analysis and modeling. To this day, we still collaborate in one-offs.
